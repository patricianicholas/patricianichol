How Long Are You Willing to Wait?

Turn your skills into an income stream and land your first paying client in 90 days.

The real problem

It's not that you're unqualified.

The problem is that you've built your entire income plan around getting a job and waiting for a monthly salary.

But what if I told you that you can work directly with business owners remotely, on your own terms, without being tied to one employer.

You work from anywhere, you get paid consistently, and you are your own boss.

Business owners constantly need support, and they will pay anyone who can step in and help them solve the problems that overwhelm them. You can support business owners remotely with tasks that save them time and help them grow their businesses. This can include email management, scheduling, social media management, customer support, data entry, research, project coordination, and so much more.

There are so many services you can offer. For example, one of the most in-demand services right now is social media management, where VAs charge anywhere from $300 to $500 per client per month.

Now imagine landing just one client paying you $300 per month. That's $300 coming in every month from one client. Now imagine having three clients paying the same amount. That's $900 per month.

Imagine having five clients paying between $300 and $500 each. That's $900 to $1,500 every single month.

The interesting thing is that you don't need years of experience or a fancy degree to get started. You just need to have a marketable skill, choose a service, learn how to deliver it well, and find people who need your help.

One question

Do you want to keep competing with thousands of applicants for a job, or learn how to position yourself as someone business owners can work with directly?

Option A

Continue waiting for a recruiter to reply, waiting for an interview invitation, waiting for someone else to decide whether you deserve an income.

Option B

Stop waiting for companies to hire you. Start creating opportunities for yourself, attracting clients, and building an income from your skills.
